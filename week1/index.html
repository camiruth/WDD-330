    SitePoint

  • Make everything designed for mobile first as most interfaces are used on phones and not computers.
  • Provide the user with what they need. Make your app quick, it changes if people stay or leave from your app. Users use one hand on a small device.
  • Speed Matters... Reduce image usage, SVG/Webfonts are lower bandwidth, Optimize and Minify CSS and JS files
  • Primary Controls.. nav bar at the bottom for more accessibility. Adding Menu instead of Hamburger will add more clicks to it.
  • Add label text inside the form instead of on the outside. Float labels help so you don't lose the text when you start typing.
  • Autofill is good for phones. If the user is entering numbers, use the number keypad. Link phone numbers to click and call instead of copy and paste.
  • Keep advertising smaller and then reveal when clicked. Do not use pop ups and popovers.
